Physics Education Research

Marshall's area of specialization is in physics education research (PER). PER is an example of discipline-based education research, in that it is generally conducted by researchers in a physics department, most often with formal academic training in physics. Physics education researchers are uniquely well-suited to study the teaching and learning of physics because they are intimately well-acquainted with the subject matter, and they are scientists first and foremost.

As the name suggests, physics education research is primarily concerned with improving the teaching and learning of physics. Learning is very complicated, so there are many sub-fields and specializations under the blanket of PER.
